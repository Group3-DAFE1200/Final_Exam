About us

Greentrotters is a company that is concerned with green traveling. Our main ambition is to provide the world with creative and user-friendly solutions on how to travel as green as possible.
In a world where climate changes threaten our very existence, we firmly believe that the key to the future lies in simple solutions for everyday people. We therefore want to develop technology that makes it easy for people to choose the greenest option for traveling.
Our company consists of four passionate technology students, deeply concerned with environmental issues.
Ingrid Marie Øiberg Lund is our manager. Her main assets are interpersonal communication and personalized leadership. She also has the general overview of the company.
Kenneth Novela Gran is our chief coder, bringing technical expertise and supreme computer knowledge into the fold.
Runa Unnsdatter Bjrønbakken Birkeland is the creative heart of the company combining graphic design with cutting edge ideas.
Astrid Pettersen is head of marketing adding her deep understanding of costumer psychology to the group.

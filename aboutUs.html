ABOUT US

Greentrotters is concerned with green traveling. Our main ambition is to provide the world with creative and user-friendly solutions to travel as green as possible.

In a world where climate changes threaten our very existence, we firmly believe that the key to the future lies in simple solutions for everyday people. We therefore want to develop technology that makes it easy for people to choose the greenest option for traveling.

Our company consists of four passionate technology students, deeply concerned with environmental issues.
